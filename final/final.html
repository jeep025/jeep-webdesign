A COLLECTION OF PHOTOS THAT I TOOK IN SEVERAL CITIES IN 2017....

在2017年，我走过了很多城市。每到一个城市我都会用相机记录下我的感受。

DALIAN
Dalian, is the city where I was born. It is a coastal city and seaport in the south of Liaoning Province, China. It is the southernmost city of the Liaodong Peninsula. Most of my memory of Dalian is the seashore, beach and sunshine. And also, my family and friends.

In 2016 summer, I arrived at Providence. The first impression of Providence is that the weather here is so similar to my hometown’s. My main memory about Providence is the overwhelming homework from school. The photo was took at Wickenden street during winter, one block away from where I live.

Boston
Due to some landscape architecture homework, I have been to Boston, especially North end area, a lot of times. In comparison to the city scale of Providence, Boston seems to be a big city. However, for me, a person who had lived in Beijing for five years for my undergraduate school, Boston is more like a medium-size district of a big city. By the way, this photo was took at Boston Public Library. I was impressed by the interior architecture by then.

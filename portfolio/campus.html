WINTER CLUB


Be familiar with different materials and various design elements (mostly natural elements, like wind and moisture)



Location: Boston(LMA AREA)

Feb.2017- May.2017

Instructor: Scheri Fultineer, Katy Flynn, Kaki Martin, Robyn Reed

Individual Work


Abstract: Not so many students would like to go outside and do outdoor activities during the winter in Boston due to the cold weather. So in this design, I am trying to encourage college students to go outside. In order to elongate the time of daytime (sunshine), I manipulate the the topography in the site, hoping more students could enjoy outside activities during the winter.

TRADITION AND MODERN


Residential Area Design



Location: Qin Huang Dao, He Bei Province, China

Mar.2015- Jun.2015

Instructor: Wang Tie, Zhong ShanFeng

Individual Work


Abstract: Nowadays, according to the research, residents in mounting numbers are not satisfied with their living conditions due to a lot of specific reasons. For example, people who work in big cities, like New York City, barely get access to the attractive scenery, such as gardens, national parks or something else. Living in the small apartments in big cities, those people can not enjoy their lives as some residents who lives at rural area. On the other hand, some residents, who live in the slums, which is common in some rual area in China, suffer from the poor living conditions also desperately want to change their living environment. Under this situation, I design a new type of community, which could improve the living condition to some extent. Besides, the site has already been designed and constructed. During the class, our professor require us to pretend the site is a blank space, starting from the scratch.

REVITALIZING BEI XIAO RIVER


Waterfront Design



Location: Bei Xiao River, Chang Yang District, Beijing, China

Sep.2014- Dec.2014

Instructor:Wu Xiangyan, Ding Yuan

Individual Work


Abstract: As the rapid developments of our cities, a great amount of problems are come up with the constructions of the metropolitans. For example, a lot of dams were built along the riverside in order to control the speed of the rivers. However, on the other hand, the existence of the dams prevent residents interact with the river. Under this situation, my design is aim to create more recreational space along the riverside in order to provide people some space to hold different kinds of activities.

REANIMATION OF XIANG TANG VILLAGE


Village/Urban Development



Location: Xiang Tang Village, Nanjing, China

Sep.2017- Dec.2017

Instructor: Michael Blier

Individual Work


Abstract: The development of urban farming is super popular in China in recent years. Almost everywhere in cities areas, roofs of buildings, backyards and green space along the street, is occupied by urban farming. However, even though urban farming benefits a lot of people in terms of fresh foods and educational purpose, it also arouses different kinds of problems, such as inappropriate and excessive use of agricultural inputs. So, in this project, I am trying to develop an improved version of urban farming.

WATER & LIGHT LAB


Be familiar with different materials and various design elements (mostly natural elements, like wind and moisture)



Location: Risd Farm

Sep.2016- Dec.2016

Instructor: Nick Depace

Individual Work


Abstract: In the Design Principle course, we are required to use different kinds of mateials to make models in order to be familiar with the different properties of the materials. Specifically, we use wire to make a soil profile, and use paper to change the pathway of the light. In addition, we design a lab in Risd farm through light, wind and tide. For example, light will cast the shadow differently in different times of one day. By modeling the landform, we make the change of the shadow obviously.
